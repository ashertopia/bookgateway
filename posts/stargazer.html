Stargazer by Patrick Carman

2011-03-06 • Arieltopia Children & Teens Fantasy

From back - When last we saw Alexa Daley, she had defeated a threat in her homeland and was sailing with Ronald Warvold across the Lonely Sea. She had no idea what adventures awaited her...until now. After a tragic attack by an evil force, Alexa and her friend Yipes are stranded on a strange community known as the Five Stone Pillars. Each pillar of rock has its own secrets...and its own challenges. As darkness descends, Alexa must risk everything to defeat it-even if it means taking to the skies.

Stargazer Land of Elyon, Book 4 by Patrick Carman Scholastic September 2008

The book was easy to read and I loved it! This is the fourth and last book (I know sad right) but this book is amazing. You do need to have read the other books to know what the book is talking about sometimes so if you cant remember what happened in the last book then its fine just make sure you reread the previous book before starting this one.

I got into the book very quickly and I was at the edge of my seat throughout the whole book. It was very suspenseful and had a lot of adventure in it. It also had a tiny bit of romance in it but nothing parents would need to worry about. I highly recommend this book because it was so much fun, I still cant believe Alexa's story is done - but it is.

The book is not obviously christian and nobody quotes the bible but it is great! I think everyone from nine to around twelve would like this book series. I recommended this book to several of my friends last week and now I see someone in every class reading the series and they all have said that they are great. The books are constantly checked out of the library!
